About Us

In this internet era, As an independent organization, We strive to bring light to the difference between reality and fake news by addressing statements issued by the public and for the public.

  • We are based in Windsor, Canada.
  • We address and verify the statements to educate our readers on the real news.
  • We work relentlessly with our sources before confirming the story.
  • Machine learning model integrated on our webpage detects the fake news and call it out.

We are a dedicated group of individuals who have taken the initiative to investigate the accuracy of statements made in the public interest or by the general public.

Our Mission

As a fact-checker organization, we want to actively monitor social media, online news/print media and pick statements given by public figures, celebrities, government officials or any particular person in power and cross-check it for our fellow readers to authenticate or rebut the claim. We would address the issue and source to prove the point. Our mission is to apply our best practices to this.

Our Plan

We plan on taking forward our fact-checking initiative to the levels of maximum reliability with our thorough research and knowledge. Every piece of content will be reviewed by machine learning tools integrated on our page, only the factual statements will be approved, and notify of the debunks. We want to speak with data and facts to show a wider effect of statements issued and well inform about the situation.

Our Vision

Reducing deception in the world has been our biggest inspiration and motivation to provide the best of the services in our knowledge to fact-check every statement that comes across our webpage. In a world, which is working all over with the use of the internet, identifying and reviewing statements that may or may not directly/indirectly impact our lives seems to be of crucial importance.
